The varied picture of controlling and coercive behaviour charges

Across England, 12 police forces out of 29 have brought fewer than one charge of controlling and coercive behaviour for every 100,000 people under their jurisdiction. Those police forces are...

  • Devon and Cornwall
  • Dorset
  • Durham
  • Essex
  • Hertfodshire
  • Lancashire
  • Leicestershire
  • Metropolitan Police
  • North Yorkshire
  • Northamptonshire
  • Thames Valley
  • Warwickshire

A further ten forces failed or refused to provide the number of charges they recorded.

Source: FOI requests made by The Bureau of Investigative Journalism
